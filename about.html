About Green Valley

Permaculture

Jane Sears is a certified Permaculture Designer who lives in Green Valley, Ontario. Jane spent many years as an operations professional, managing people over a wide geographic region and spending a lot of time on the road away from her family. In searching for a more organic life Jane was first introduced to permaculture a few years ago and began reading about this holistic version of land management that resonated with her. This led her to take a Permaculture Design course and upon completion she started applying the principles to Treverva Farm, a small CSA vegetable farm run by Jane and her husband John. Land that was very difficult to grow on is now producing an abundance of fruit and vegetables without pesticides or any outside inputs. The farm is a testament to what is possible and is a great example of how permaculture can change land in a positive way. The change for Jane did not just apply to the farm but was the catalyst for Jane to walk away from a very successful professional career and focus full time on sharing with others the joy and peace she has found in living a simpler, more authentic life. Jane will partner with you to show you what is possible and help you achieve your food security goals.
