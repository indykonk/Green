Weekly Seasonal CSA Shares

We grow a variety of vegetables and fruit for your table from May to October (weather dependent). We don't use any pesticides and use a no-till method to ensure we are putting more into the soil than we take out. We use a closed-loup system that ensures we maximize every input available. We also chop and drop as we look after the plants and trees to return nutrients to the soil and we compost onsite to ensure our ecological footprint remains as small as possible.

Frequently Asked Questions

What if the design you provide is too expensive to implement?

Self sustainability is not achieved in one growing season and so we work with you to provide a project plan that meets your budget. This can include focusing on one area of your land at a time, or even one raised bed at a time. Your budget and the amount of time you have available are part of the discussion we have prior to completing a design for your land.

I am new to gardening and don't know how to grow my own food?

We start simple. Through a series of questions I determine what food types you like and we start with those. If you have never grown anything before we will start you off with a few plants that require less maintenance until you gain confidence. Like anything new we encourage you to start small.

I live in a cold climate and cannot grow all year outside so how can I achieve self sufficiency?

We offer many solutions to extend the growing season to give you the opportunity to maximize what you can grow. We also make use of growing opportunities inside the home. In addition we offer suggestions on how to manage your harvest and store food for the winter months when you cannot grow outside. Part of sustainable living means eating seasonally and preserving your harvest which may mean changing your cooking habits. We are happy to provide information that will help you achieve this.

You offer a service that does not require you to come to my location so how can you create a design?

We use Google Earth to get an overhead view of your location. We also use online mapping tools that provide additional information and we ask you to provide pictures from different angles. We then research the sun-line and wind-lines of your location that enables us to gather the information we need to create a design that is unique to your property.

I am a renter so I do not want to spend lots of money on a property I may not live in for a long time.

We fully understand that as a renter you may want to ensure you are not spending money on a property you may not get to enjoy for years so we look at more portable solutions. We look at container growing and raised beds that you can unscrew and take with you when you move. We also look at what space you have indoors to grow, for example your herbs. There are lots of great ideas that work for renters.
